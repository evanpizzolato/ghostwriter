Ghostwriter is a privacy-first notes app for presenters. Your notes stay invisible to screenshots, screen recordings, and screen shares so you can present with confidence.

Always-on-top. Click-through mode. Global shortcuts. A minimal, distraction-free editor that gets out of your way and keeps your secrets safe.
